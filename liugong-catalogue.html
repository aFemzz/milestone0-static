Liugong, officially known as Guangxi Liugong Machinery Co., Ltd., is a leading Chinese construction machinery manufacturer with a global presence.

The company specializes in producing a wide range of machinery, including excavators, wheel loaders, bulldozers, motor graders, skid steer loaders, forklifts, and more.

Through strategic partnerships and a comprehensive distribution network, Liugong serves customers in over 100 countries, providing them with reliable machinery and comprehensive support services.
